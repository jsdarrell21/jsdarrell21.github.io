Small Child, Big Dreams

As a child using my imagination to be able to create things as I wished them to be always amazed me. With such toys as legos, I could bring an image I had in my mind to life. There was always a large amount of satisfaction I felt in seeing my masterpieces exist in the real world. I new some day I'd find something I could apply this logic to when I grew up.

Having found myself fall into the tracks of the coding route caught me off guard. Little had I realized there was such an amazing world out there I had been blind to for such a long time.
